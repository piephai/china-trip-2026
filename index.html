Hiking Routes

Route: Forest Park entrance ‚Üí Golden Whip Stream (2-3hr, 7.5km) ‚Üí Climb to Yuanjiajie via Qianlixianghui trail (1hr steep stairs) ‚Üí Yuanjiajie viewpoints
üí° Watch for wild monkeys along Golden Whip Stream - don't carry visible food!
